Directions
Ingredients

  • 1 cup Crushed Graham Crackers
  • 1/2 cup Melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cup Coconut
  • 1 12 oz. bag Chocolate chips
  • 1 12 oz. bag Butterscotch chips
  • 1 1/2 cup Chopped walnuts
  • 1 10 oz. bag Miniature marshmallows
  • 1 can Sweetened condensed milk
Example


    • Preheat oven to 350 degrees
    • Crush graham crackers and combine with melted butter. Press into 9 by 13 pan.
    • Layer coconut on top of graham crust, followed by both kinds of chips, walnuts, and marshmallows.
    • Drizzle entire can of sweetened condensed milk on top of marshmallows.
    • Bake uncovered for 15 minutes or until marshmallows start to brown.
